Frequently Asked Questions

It's easy! Browse stores by category or use the search bar to find what you need. Add items to your cart, choose a delivery address, select your payment method (San Cash wallet, credit/debit card, or cash on delivery), and confirm your order. You'll receive real-time updates as your order is prepared and delivered.
MyPlopPlop accepts multiple payment methods for your convenience: San Cash wallet (our built-in digital wallet), credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), mobile money (MonCash, Natcom), and cash on delivery for local orders. Diaspora customers can pay with international cards.
San Cash is MyPlopPlop's built-in digital wallet. You can top it up using a credit/debit card, mobile money, or bank transfer. Once loaded, use it for fast one-tap checkout on any order. San Cash also supports receiving funds from family abroad, earning cashback on orders, and transferring balance to other MyPlopPlop users.

If you're in the diaspora (USA, Canada, France, etc.), you can order groceries, medicine, food, and more for your family and friends in Haiti. Joining as a merchant is simple. Sign up for a merchant account, provide your business details (name, location, category), upload your menu or product catalog, and submit for verification. Once approved (usually within 24-48 hours), your store will be live on MyPlopPlop. You'll get access to a merchant dashboard to manage orders, products, and earnings.
To become a MyPlopPlop rider, sign up with a rider account and provide: a valid ID, proof of vehicle (motorcycle or car), and a phone number. After a brief verification process, you can start accepting delivery requests. You'll earn per delivery plus tips, and you can cash out your earnings daily to your San Cash wallet or mobile money account.
Delivery fees vary based on distance and merchant. Typically, fees range from HTG 50 to HTG 250 depending on how far the delivery location is from the store. Many merchants offer free delivery on orders above a certain amount. New customers also get free delivery on their first order using the code WELCOME.
Once your order is confirmed, you can track it in real time from the Orders tab. You'll see each step: order received, merchant preparing, rider picked up, and out for delivery. You'll also receive SMS notifications at each stage. The live map shows your rider's location so you always know when to expect your delivery.
If there's an issue with your order (wrong items, missing items, quality problems), you can request a refund directly from the order details page within 24 hours of delivery. Go to Orders > Select Order > Report Issue. Our support team reviews each request and typically processes refunds within 1-2 business days. Refunds are credited to your San Cash wallet or original payment method.
You can reach our support team through multiple channels: WhatsApp (fastest, available 7am-10pm daily), email at support@myplopplop.com, or through the in-app chat. For order-specific issues, use the "Report Issue" button on the order details page for the fastest resolution. Our average response time is under 15 minutes during business hours.
