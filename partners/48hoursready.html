Questions

FAQ

How much does it cost to join?
Signing up on the marketplace is free. 48HoursReady website packages are separate and depend on your needs. MyPlopPlop takes a small commission on each order — you only pay when you make money.
How long until my store is live?
Your marketplace listing can be live within 24 hours of approval. Your 48HoursReady website typically takes 48-72 hours depending on complexity.
How do I receive payments?
Choose your preferred method during signup: MonCash, NatCash, San Cash wallet, or bank transfer. Payouts are processed regularly through your merchant dashboard.
Do I need to handle deliveries myself?
No. PlopPlop SouWout drivers handle pickup and delivery. You just prepare the order and mark it ready in your dashboard. The driver comes to you.
Can I manage my own products and prices?
Absolutely. Your merchant dashboard lets you add, edit, or remove products, update prices, set availability, and control your store hours anytime.
What areas do you deliver to?
We currently serve Port-au-Prince metro (Petion-Ville, Delmas, Tabarre, Carrefour, Kenscoff, Croix-des-Bouquets) with expansion to Cap-Haitien, Les Cayes, and Jacmel coming soon.
